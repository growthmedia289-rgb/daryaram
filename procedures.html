Advanced Pain Management Procedures

Evidence-based minimally invasive procedures for effective pain relief, improved mobility, and enhanced quality of life.

Comprehensive Pain Assessment

Every treatment begins with a detailed evaluation to identify the underlying cause of pain. Our specialists review your medical history, perform a thorough physical examination, and assess previous investigations before recommending a personalized treatment plan.

Medication-Based Pain Management

Many painful conditions can be effectively managed with carefully selected medications. Treatment plans are tailored to each patient's condition, severity of symptoms, and overall health, with regular follow-up to monitor progress.

Therapeutic Pain Injections

For patients with persistent or severe pain, minimally invasive injection therapies may be recommended. These procedures help reduce inflammation, relieve pain, and improve mobility while minimizing the need for surgery whenever appropriate.

Joint Pain Management

We provide non-surgical treatment for painful joints caused by arthritis, inflammation, injury, or age-related degeneration. Management may include medications, therapeutic injections, rehabilitation, and lifestyle guidance.

Spine & Back Pain Management

Our pain clinic offers comprehensive care for cervical pain, back pain, sciatica, slipped disc-related pain, and other spinal conditions. Treatment focuses on reducing pain, restoring function, and improving quality of life through conservative and interventional approaches.

Nerve Pain Management

We evaluate and treat various forms of chronic nerve pain, including conditions such as trigeminal neuralgia, post-herpetic neuralgia, and other neuropathic pain disorders using evidence-based pain management strategies.

Physiotherapy & Rehabilitation

Rehabilitation plays an important role in long-term pain recovery. Our physiotherapy programs focus on strengthening muscles, improving flexibility, restoring movement, correcting posture, and reducing the risk of recurrence.
